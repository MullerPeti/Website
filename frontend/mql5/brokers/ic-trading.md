# IC Trading

**Website:** https://www.ictrading.com/?camp=88790

## Short summary

IC Trading is a forex and CFD broker offering MT4 and MT5, primarily positioned toward traders seeking low trading costs, broad instrument coverage, and support for automated trading strategies.

## Broker overview

IC Trading operates as a multi-entity broker serving retail clients across different regions.  
Its offering is focused on providing access to major forex pairs, indices, commodities, and CFDs under relatively competitive pricing conditions.

The broker is commonly used by traders running expert advisors and systematic strategies, particularly those that are cost-sensitive.

## Trading platforms

- MetaTrader 4  
- MetaTrader 5  
- TradingView
- CTrader

Both platforms support full expert advisor functionality, custom indicators, and automated trading scripts.  
Execution quality is generally considered suitable for retail algorithmic trading, including short-term and systematic approaches.
