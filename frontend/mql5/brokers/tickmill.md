# Tickmill

**Website:** https://www.tickmill.com/

## Short summary

Tickmill is an MT4/MT5 broker known for relatively low trading costs and execution conditions that are commonly used by scalpers and automated trading systems.

## Broker overview

Tickmill operates multiple regulated entities, allowing it to serve both EU and international retail clients.  
The broker is frequently chosen by traders who prioritize tight spreads, transparent pricing, and straightforward execution over extensive product variety.

Its overall positioning is focused on execution efficiency rather than platform innovation or ancillary services.

## Trading platforms

- MetaTrader 4  
- MetaTrader 5  
- Web-based trading interface  

Both MT4 and MT5 support full expert advisor functionality, custom indicators, and automated trading workflows.  
Execution quality is generally suitable for short-term and systematic trading, including scalping, within retail market constraints.

## Regulation and legal structure

Tickmill operates under the following regulators:

- Financial Conduct Authority (United Kingdom)  
- Cyprus Securities and Exchange Commission (CySEC)  
- Financial Services Authority (Seychelles)  

EU clients are onboarded under the CySEC-regulated entity and are subject to ESMA leverage limits and investor protection rules.  
US residents are not accepted.

## Costs and execution

Trading costs at Tickmill are typically **low**, particularly on commission-based account types:

- Tight spreads on major FX pairs  
- Clear commission structures on raw accounts  
- Execution suitable for cost-sensitive and short-term strategies  

As with all brokers, execution quality and spreads depend on market conditions and account configuration.

## Suitable use cases

Tickmill may be appropriate for:

- Automated trading using MT4 or MT5  
- Scalping and other short-term strategies  
- Cost-sensitive systematic trading  
- Trading major FX pairs and indices  

It may be less suitable for:

- Traders seeking a very broad CFD product range  
- Long-term investing-oriented workflows  

## User base and reputation

Tickmill reports a global user base in the range of **500k+ clients**.  
Public feedback often highlights pricing and execution, with mixed opinions on platform features and regional support.

As with all brokers, trading conditions depend on jurisdiction, regulatory entity, and account type.
