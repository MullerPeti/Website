# XM Group

**Website:** https://www.xm.com/

## Short summary

XM Group is a large global retail forex and CFD broker offering MT4 and MT5, with a strong focus on accessibility, broad market coverage, and multi-jurisdictional regulation.

## Broker overview

XM operates through several regulated entities, serving retail clients across Europe and many non-EU regions.  
The broker is widely known for its large client base, simplified onboarding process, and extensive educational and promotional ecosystem.

Its positioning is oriented toward mass retail participation rather than execution-optimized or institution-style trading.

## Trading platforms

- MetaTrader 4  
- MetaTrader 5  
- Web-based trading interface  

MT4 and MT5 are supported with standard functionality, including expert advisors, indicators, and automated trading scripts.  
Execution quality is generally stable for retail trading, though not specifically optimized for latency-sensitive or high-frequency strategies.

## Regulation and legal structure

XM operates under the following regulators:

- Cyprus Securities and Exchange Commission (CySEC)  
- Australian Securities and Investments Commission (ASIC)  
- International Financial Services Commission (Belize)  

EU clients are onboarded under the CySEC-regulated entity and are subject to ESMA leverage limits and investor protection rules.  
US residents are not accepted.

## Costs and execution

Trading costs at XM are typically **medium**:

- Mostly spread-based pricing models  
- Spreads are wider than ECN-focused brokers  
- Execution is reliable but not designed for cost-critical strategies  

Conditions may vary depending on account type and regulatory entity.

## Suitable use cases

XM may be appropriate for:

- Swing trading on forex and indices  
- Lower-frequency automated strategies  
- Grid and hedging approaches with moderate cost sensitivity  
- Traders prioritizing platform familiarity and broad instrument access  

It is generally less suitable for:

- High-frequency scalping  
- Cost-sensitive algorithmic trading  
- Traders seeking institutional-grade execution
