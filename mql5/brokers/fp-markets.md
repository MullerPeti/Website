# FP Markets

**Website:** https://www.fpmarkets.com/

## Short summary

FP Markets is an MT4/MT5 broker known for relatively strong execution quality and competitive pricing, making it a common choice for algorithmic and short-term trading strategies.

## Broker overview

FP Markets operates as a multi-regulated broker with a focus on providing access to forex and CFD markets under a relatively transparent pricing structure.  
The broker is frequently used by traders running expert advisors and systematic strategies due to its platform support and execution characteristics.

Client conditions, leverage, and available instruments depend on the regulatory entity and jurisdiction.

## Trading platforms

- MetaTrader 4  
- MetaTrader 5  
- cTrader  
- Web-based trading interface  

MT4 and MT5 support full expert advisor functionality, custom indicators, and automated trading workflows.  
cTrader is also available for traders who prefer an alternative execution and charting environment.

Execution is generally suitable for retail algorithmic trading, including scalping and news-related strategies, within broker-imposed limits.

## Regulation and legal structure

FP Markets operates under the following regulators:

- Australian Securities and Investments Commission (ASIC)  
- Cyprus Securities and Exchange Commission (CySEC)  

EU clients are onboarded under the CySEC-regulated entity and are subject to ESMA rules, including leverage restrictions and negative balance protection.  
US residents are not accepted.

## Costs and execution

Trading costs at FP Markets are typically **low**, especially on commission-based account types:

- Competitive raw spreads on major FX pairs  
- Transparent commission structures  
- Execution conditions suitable for cost-sensitive strategies  

Costs and execution quality can vary depending on account type and regulatory entity.

## Suitable use cases

FP Markets may be appropriate for:

- Automated trading using MT4 or MT5  
- Scalping and short-term strategies  
- News-driven trading under defined risk controls  
- Systematic trading requiring relatively tight spreads  

It may be less suitable for:

- Traders seeking proprietary or non-MetaTrader platforms only  
- Long-term investing-focused workflows  

## User base and reputation

FP Markets reports a global user base in the range of **300k+ clients**.  
Public feedback often highlights execution quality and pricing, with mixed opinions on support and regional onboarding processes.

As with all brokers, trading conditions depend on jurisdiction, entity, and selected account configuration.
