# FBS

**Website:** https://fbs.com/

## Short summary

FBS is a high-volume retail broker offering MT4 and MT5, with a particularly strong presence in emerging markets and a large global client base.

## Broker overview

FBS operates through both EU-regulated and offshore entities, allowing it to serve a wide range of international retail traders.  
The broker is known for accessibility, simplified account structures, and broad market reach rather than institutional-style trading conditions.

Its offering is often oriented toward newer traders and lower-capital accounts, though MT4 and MT5 support allows for basic automated trading.

## Trading platforms

- MetaTrader 4  
- MetaTrader 5  
- Web-based trading interface  

MT4 and MT5 are supported with standard functionality, including expert advisors and custom indicators.  
Execution quality is generally sufficient for swing trading and lower-frequency automated strategies, but not optimized for latency-sensitive trading.

## Regulation and legal structure

FBS operates under the following regulators:

- Cyprus Securities and Exchange Commission (CySEC)  
- International Financial Services Commission (Belize)  

EU clients are onboarded through the CySEC-regulated entity and are subject to ESMA rules, including leverage caps and mandatory risk protections.  
US residents are not accepted.

## Costs and execution

Trading costs at FBS are typically **medium**, depending on account type and regulatory entity:

- Spreads are generally wider than ECN-focused brokers  
- Commission-free pricing is common  
- Execution is stable but not designed for high-frequency trading  

Conditions can vary significantly between EU and offshore accounts.

## Suitable use cases

FBS may be appropriate for:

- Swing trading on forex and indices  
- Lower-frequency automated strategies  
- Traders prioritizing ease of access and platform familiarity  
- Grid or hedging strategies with moderate cost sensitivity  

It is generally less suitable for:

- High-frequency scalping  
- Cost-critical algorithmic systems  
- Traders requiring institutional-style execution

## User base and reputation

FBS reports a global user base exceeding **20M+ registered clients**, largely concentrated in emerging markets.  
Public feedback often reflects mixed experiences, with positive comments on accessibility and negative feedback related to spreads and regional conditions.

As with all brokers, trading conditions depend on jurisdiction, entity, and account configuration.
