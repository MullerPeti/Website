# Trade Manager Interface MT5

**Trade Manager Interface MT5** is a **free, lightweight utility** built to simplify and speed up **manual trading** in MetaTrader 5. It focuses on **risk control, visual clarity, and execution precision**, making professional trade management accessible directly from the chart.

> MT4 version available  
> Please leave a review if you find the tool useful.

---

## What It Does

The Trade Manager Interface allows you to **plan, execute, and manage trades visually**—without manual calculations or repetitive actions.  
It is designed purely for **manual traders** who want structure, speed, and consistency.

For advanced hedging, grid, and recovery workflows, see **ManHedger**.

---

## Key Features

- Two operation modes: **Trade** and **Close**
- Full **visual trade setup** on the chart  
  (Entry, Stop Loss, Take Profit)
- **Automatic position sizing**:
  - Fixed lots
  - % of balance
  - Fixed monetary amount (margin-based)
- Stop Loss & Take Profit shown in:
  - Points
  - Percentage
  - Price levels
- **Automatic Breakeven**
- **Trailing Stop**
- Fixed **Risk/Reward ratio lock**
- Up to **3 Partial Take Profits**
  - Custom distances
  - Custom volumes
- **Drag-and-adjust** trade levels directly on the chart

---

## How It Works

1. Open the **Trade Manager panel** on the chart  
2. Select direction: **Buy** or **Sell**  
3. Define Entry, Stop Loss, Take Profit, and Volume  
4. Optionally enable:
   - Auto Breakeven
   - Trailing Stop
   - Partial Take Profits
5. Click **Open Trade**

The EA automatically places a **market or pending order** based on the configured parameters.

### Visual Lines

- Enable **Display Lines** to view and modify trade levels on the chart  
- Moving a line instantly updates point and percentage values in the panel  
- ⚠️ Trades are executed based on **panel parameters**, not line positions

---

## Close Mode

Close Mode provides **fast trade and order control** using dedicated buttons:

- Close ALL
- Close Open positions
- Close Profitable trades
- Close Losing trades
- Delete Pending orders
